Rolling Game

A very simple game where you roll a ball and collect coins. It's pretty much the game made by BornCG in his Godot 3D game tutorial. I mostly just followed their tutorial to become acquainted with the Godot engine, but I ended up having some fun adding my own stuff to it and improving some of the systems.

You can play the game in your browser here. Alternatively, you can download a Windows version of the game here.
